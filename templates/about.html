About Review | Alliance

Here at Review | Alliance we love writing about music! Our goal is to bring our site visitors the 100% honest truth about what is worth listening to and what is worth seeing! If you want to be reviewed, please contact us through social media (see links in the footer). Anyhow, Review | Alliance started the journey 2020 and then consisted of Marcus and Ulrika but the family grows! Do you want to join us? Don't hesitate to sign up!
